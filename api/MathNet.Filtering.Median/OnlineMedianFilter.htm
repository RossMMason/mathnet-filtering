Type OnlineMedianFilter

Namespace MathNet.Filtering.Median

Parent OnlineFilter

Median-Filters are non-linear filters, returning the median of a sample window as output. Median-Filters perform well for de-noise applications where it's important to not loose sharp steps/edges.
